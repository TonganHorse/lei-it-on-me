Who are we?

We are from what is known as the "friendly islands" aka Tonga. We had one purpose in mind when creating Lei It On Me and that was to provide meaning to Leis. We want to invite all to learn the significance of them and how they can bring light to any occasion. Many have heard of Leis but many do not know what they truly mean. They are a symbol of love. They are also a symbol of great accomplishment to those who wear them. They are an omen of good will that once was shared in the islands to foreigners and locals alike and now can also be shared by you!
