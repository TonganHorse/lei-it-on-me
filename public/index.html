Want to give a unique and different gift that stands out from every other gift? Leis are a wonderful alternative to traditional gifts. A lei is a symbol of love, friendship, celebration, honor or greeting. Leis are common at graduations and for celebrations and parties. School dances and weddings are the perfect times for leis, and leis are even in use in the office to honor someone and celebrate achievements.
